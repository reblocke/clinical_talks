# Anaphylaxis and Angioedema

## Angioedema

![alt](https://photos.collectednotes.com/photos/5187/06657f72-c3be-4130-92cb-80b80b2a67d5)

Dermal, SQ, mucosal swelling - non pitting. vasodilation and increased vascular permeability.

24-72 hour course

- idiopathic
- associated with allergic and non-allergic reactions due to antigens (e.g. venom, drugs, foods, pathogens, animals, latex(
- C1-INH deficiency: inherited or acquired 

![alt](https://photos.collectednotes.com/photos/5187/571cbeb7-2efd-4a67-9fcc-56d8c7230ba0)
