# vasopressors

- Venous vasoconstriction = most important. Less important to increase SVR
-NE: less splachnic/myocardial ischemia than epi. less digit ischemia than vasopressin. Diminishing returns after 1-2 mcg/kg/min

## When to start
Some evidence (earlier normalization of perfusion indices) for earlier than hypotension despite 30cc/kg bolus cyrstalloid: CENSER trial DOI: 10.1164/rccm.201806-1034OC
