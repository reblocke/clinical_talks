# Pacemakers




### Leadless pacemaker
- self contained generator and electrode that is implanted into the right ventricle for single chamber pacing. 
- benefit: no pocket infections, pacer wires, no incision (implanted via femoral vein), MRI compatible
- down side: 1 chamber, can't defibrillate 

(Citation: The leadless pacemaker: An innovative design to enhance pacemaking capabilities. JAAPA 2019;32:48-50.)
